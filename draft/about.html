The idea for this project came about when I began researching how climate change would affect housing in the United States. I stumbled across a recently published article that used machine learning in combination with other migration models to predict net migration by county in the United States by 2100.

I created this dashboard using the Urban Institute Data Visualization Style Guide as well as the r packages urbanmapr and urbanthemes which are opensource. I think that they make this page look lovely. I also used shiny, the tidyverse, and gt packages for the web app, data manipulation and tables respectively.
